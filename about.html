About TeddyTech

Driving digital transformation through innovation and education

Our Story

Founded in 2023, TeddyTech began as a small initiative to bridge the digital skills gap in our community. What started as weekend coding workshops has now grown into a full-fledged technology hub offering comprehensive training and digital solutions.

We believe in empowering individuals and organizations through technology education and innovative software solutions tailored to local needs.

Our Mission

To democratize access to quality technology education and provide affordable, world-class digital solutions to businesses and educational institutions.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate technologists, educators, and designers committed to making a difference through technology.
